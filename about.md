## About Me

Hi, I'm **Jinseok Kim (Joshua)** — an **Analytics Engineer** with **4+ years** of hands-on experience across the full data spectrum: from analysis to engineering to AI development.

I build **end-to-end data pipelines**, design **dbt-based data models**, and develop **AI-powered tools** that empower teams to make smarter decisions. My career has taken me through Korea's leading tech companies and a U.S. fintech startup, giving me a unique cross-cultural perspective on solving data problems at scale.
