<h2>About Me</h2>

Hi, I'm **Jinseok Kim (Joshua)** — an **Analytics Engineer** with **4+ years** of hands-on experience across the full data spectrum: from analysis to engineering to AI development.

I design **scalable, usable, and cost-efficient data pipelines** grounded in a strong understanding of data's business value. My career has taken me through South Korea's leading tech companies and a U.S. fintech startup, giving me a unique cross-cultural perspective on solving data problems at scale.

I'm deeply interested in the **"Data as a Product"** philosophy — treating data assets with the same rigor, usability, and user-centricity as any customer-facing product. My aspiration is to become an **AI-native full-stack data builder** — someone who not only builds robust data infrastructure but also leverages AI to unlock new possibilities across the entire data lifecycle.

</section>
